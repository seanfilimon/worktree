## A New Era of Version Control

Today, we're thrilled to introduce **W0rkTree** — a version control system built from the ground up for modern development workflows.

## Why W0rkTree?

While Git has served us well for over a decade, modern development teams face challenges that Git wasn't designed to solve:

- **Monorepo complexity** — Large codebases need better isolation between teams
- **Manual workflows** — Staging and committing should be automatic
- **Limited permissions** — Teams need fine-grained access control
- **Dependency blindness** — Changes in one area should surface impacts elsewhere
- **Lack of Identity** — No built-in way to manage organizations and cross-tenant collaboration
- **Honor-System Licensing** — No way to technically enforce open-source or proprietary licenses

## What's Different

W0rkTree introduces several breakthrough concepts:

### Nested Trees
Each team gets their own isolated tree within the repository, with independent branches and history.

### Automatic Tracking
A background worker watches your filesystem and captures changes automatically, so you never lose work.

### Staged Visibility
W0rkTree syncs your in-progress work to the server as Staged Snapshots, giving your team real-time visibility into who is working on what without polluting your branch history.

### Multi-Tenancy & Access Control
W0rkTree natively understands Identity and Access Management (IAM), allowing you to securely share specific trees or branches with contractors, partners, or other tenants using declarative policy-as-code.

### License Compliance
W0rkTree enforces software licenses at the protocol level. If a developer tries to export or fork proprietary code without the proper license grant, the engine physically blocks it.
